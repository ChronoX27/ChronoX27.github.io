üçì Raspberry Pi

At the moment I mainly run two services on my Rasperry Pi (exact model: Raspberry Pi 4B with 2 GB Ram): Firstly a Mysterium Node, i.e. a node for a decentralized VPN. For each session I am rewarded with a few cents of a crypto-currency called MYST. The other service is a Pi-hole, an adblocking DNS server, which uses a DNS sinkhole to prevent websites from being loaded from the server in the first place.

üå± Blockchain

In my opinion, Crypto, Blockchain and NFTs offers many great opportunities, which is why I follow some Crypto-/ NFT-projects very enthusiastically (especially Mirandus, an RPG by GalaGames, where nearly everything is a NFT). However, I think that the cryptospace is too often seen from a commercial point of view. Most coins and NFTs are bought in the hope they will generate the largest possible profit. That's sad, because this makes them too expensive and they can no longer fulfil their original purpose.
Personaly I own several NFTs and coins (mainly BAT, GALA and MYST) through various chains. At the moment their value adds up to around 70-80 $ for the NFTs and about 30 $ for my coins.
